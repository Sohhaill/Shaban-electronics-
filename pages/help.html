FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free home delivery?

+

Yes! We offer free home delivery across Rawalpindi and Islamabad for purchases above PKR 5,000. For areas outside these cities, a small delivery fee may apply. Contact us for details.

What warranty do your products come with?

+

All our products come with the official manufacturer's warranty — typically 1 to 3 years depending on the brand and product type. We also provide our own after-sale support for the warranty period.

Can I buy on installments?

+

Absolutely! We offer 0% markup installment plans through several leading banks including Meezan Bank, HBL, and Bank Alfalah. Plans range from 3 to 24 months. Visit us or call for more information.

Do you install the appliances at home?

+

Yes, we provide professional installation for all appliances including ACs, washing machines, and more. Our certified technicians ensure safe and proper installation. AC installation is free with purchase.

Can I exchange my old appliance?

+

Yes! We have an old appliance exchange program where you can trade in your old unit and get a good deduction on your new purchase. Call us to get an evaluation of your old appliance.

What brands do you carry?

+

We carry all major Pakistani and international brands including Haier, Dawlance, PEL, Samsung, LG, Gree, Orient, GFC, TCL, Hisense, and many more. We are an authorized dealer for most of these brands.

Do you repair appliances from other brands?

+

Yes, our technicians are trained to repair most major home appliance brands. We provide repair services for ACs, refrigerators, washing machines, TVs, and other appliances at affordable rates.

What are your store hours?

+

Our store is open Saturday to Thursday, 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We are closed on Fridays. You can always reach us via WhatsApp or call for urgent inquiries even outside store hours.
